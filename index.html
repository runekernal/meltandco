Our Story

Crafting moments of pure delight, one waffle at a time.

A Passion for Sweetness

At Melt & Co, we believe that dessert is not just food, but an experience. Born from a passion for exquisite flavors and textures, we bring you the finest Belgian waffles, fluffy pancakes, and artisanal desserts.

Our chefs use only premium ingredients to ensure that every bite is a journey of taste. Whether you crave the crunch of a bubble waffle or the smoothness of our signature chocolate blends, Melt & Co is your sanctuary of sweetness.

- The Melt & Co Team
